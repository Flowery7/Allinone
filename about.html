Our Story

Founded in 2025 by John Smith, All In One Property Care began as a small handyman service with just one truck and a vision for quality property maintenance.

What started as a one-person operation has grown into the region's most trusted property maintenance company, with over 50 skilled professionals serving hundreds of satisfied clients annually.
